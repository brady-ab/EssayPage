Armchair Directing

Back

A lot of the time you'll watch a trailer and think a horror movie looks incredibly unique, and will scare you in ways you haven't been scared before. Upon watching it, you find that it's just like every other horror movie, and you forget it two days later. Some movies have found a solution.

Fear of the unknown. A lot of the time, our minds will scare ourselves with the sheer possibility of what could be going on far more than a detailed explanation of horrific events could. By never fully tipping their hand, the movie can accomplish two things:

Exploit the human brain's desire for an answer to every question in a movie, and continue to cause scare with fear of the unknown.
