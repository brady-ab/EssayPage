You may wonder if Esports will ever control a share in mainstream media that rivals regular sports.
I am convinced they will. Here's why:

It's no secret that video games have risen in popularity since their inception, and will continue to do so.
Because everyone owns a phone and a computer, the barrier to entry is lower than ever.
Additionally, people have never spent more time online than they do now, meaning they're more likely
to be exposed to Esports.
I think Esports' biggest threat is how fragmented and individual the competitive communities for each
game are under the umbrella that is esports.
But this problem will also solve itself, as an esport only dies when people stop playing it,
and as established, people have never been flocking to esports more.
