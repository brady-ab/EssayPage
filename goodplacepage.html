If you haven't seen The Good Place, I don't recommened reading this page.
But I do recommened watching The Good Place. Spoilers Below.

I don't think this show gets enough credit. It is generally well-liked, and has good reviews. On paper, season 2 should have been impossible after the twist they left us with in season 1. Revealing one of the main characters is a demon working against the others, and then wiping everyones memories is a surefire way to alienate the audience.
How did they make it work? Not only work, but work so well that the show is consistently amazing?
